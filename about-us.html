ABOUT METADEK

We are manufacturers of aluminum components duly anodized, hard anodized, electro colored or powder coated. We have facilities to make CNC machined components, sheet metal parts or parts made from aluminum extrusions. Our in house capability to perform various mechanical operations such as CNC machining, bending of profiles, CNC drilling, aluminum welding, buffing with various finishes, blanking, bending and forming of sheet metal parts makes us the ultimate choice for your requirement of aluminum parts.
